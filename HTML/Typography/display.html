What's a Display font?

Display fonts are well, fonts to display things, more specifically they are used for headlines; they're best for you to use at 30 pixels or more.

Display typographyies will often have more eccentric and variable designs than the simple, relatively restrained typographies generally used for body text.

One flaw about the Display font is that it is really difficult to read when its pixel size is small. You can compare the two passages below using 2 different font types (Display font and Serif font).

Evidently, anyone who can see properly would agree that the paragraph written in the serif font is easier to read than the display font.

For design, if you need a font for your header that stands out of the crowd and captures your users attention, then definitely go for a display font.
